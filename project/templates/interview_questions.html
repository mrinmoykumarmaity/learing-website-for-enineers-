Interview Tips

  • 1. Understand the Fundamentals: Solid understanding of OOP concepts is crucial for most tech interviews.
  • 2. Practice Coding: Be ready to code solutions on a whiteboard or online IDE.
  • 3. Think Out Loud: Explain your thought process as you work through problems.
  • 4. Ask Clarifying Questions: Do not assume. Ask the interviewer for clarification.
  • 5. Optimize Solutions: Start with a working solution, then optimize for time and space complexity.
